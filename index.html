What is Helix AI?

An AI-driven pipeline that uses deep learning and unsupervised learning to identify eukaryotic taxa and assess biodiversity directly from raw eDNA reads.

Explore the Depths

The deep ocean is one of the most mysterious and least explored regions on our planet. It is home to an enormous amount of biodiversity, but because of its depth and remoteness, studying it using traditional methods is extremely challenging.

Environmental DNA, or eDNA, has emerged as a powerful tool for exploring these ecosystems. By analyzing traces of genetic material left in seawater and sediments, we can detect organisms without disturbing their fragile habitats.

Our Pipeline combines classification with clustering, ensuring that we don’t just identify known organisms, but also highlight entirely new ones, while saving computation time in the process.
